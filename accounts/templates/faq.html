FAQs

How does the Artifacts process work?

Our digital platform is secure and efficient, combining cutting-edge technology with expertise to enhance the artifacts transaction process.

What kind of artifacts do you sell?

We sell a broad mix of commercial and residential properties from houses and apartments to retail units, offices, industrial units and developments sites, as well as large-scale investment portfolios.

How does bidding work?

All lots offered on Artifacts Auctions have a reserve price. The reserve price must be met in order for the work to be sold.

Why did the auction end after its original close time?

If a bid is placed 5 minutes before the auction is set to close, the auction closing will be extended by 5 minutes.

Is there a fee to buy?

Artifacts collects a 20% Buyer's Premium on all lots.
